About Me

I'm a first year PhD student at Télécom Paris - Institut Polytechnique de Paris in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, I'm part of the Signal Statistics and Machine Learning - S2A team . I'm under the supervision of Prof. Florence d'Alché-Buc and Prof. Pavlo Mozharovskyi.

Research Topic

My research focuses on developing machine learning methods for analyzing complex data structures, particularly graphs. I am especially interested in integrating uncertainty quantification techniques—such as multivariate quantile regression—into graph representation learning. My work spans several areas, including:

  • Optimal Transport
  • Kernel Methods
  • Statistical Learning
  • Deep Learning

Prior to my PhD, I explored a little bit of quantum computing, mainly quantum algorithms (QSVT) and quantum error correction.
