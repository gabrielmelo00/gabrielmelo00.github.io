About Me

I'm a first year PhD student at Télécom Paris - Institut Polytechnique de Paris in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, I'm part of the Signal Statistics and Machine Learning - S2A team . I'm under the supervision of Florence d'Alché-Buc and Pavlo Mozharovskyi.

Research Topic

My project focuses on the development of machine learning methods for the analysis of complex data structures, such as graphs. I am particularly interested in the integration of uncertainty quantification techniques, such as multivariate quantile. My works involves the span of Optimal Transport, Kernel Methods and Deep Learning.
